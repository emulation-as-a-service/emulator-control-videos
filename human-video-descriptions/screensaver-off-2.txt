RIght-click on the desktop background to bring up its context menu. Click on the most bottom entry labeled "Properties". This will open the "Display Properties" window. In the top row of tabs, click on "Screen Saver." You will then be able to seelct a "Screen saver" from a dropmenu, chose the option "[None]". The click the "Power" button in the lower part of the window. This will open the window "Power Option Properties". Select "Always On" from the "Power schemes" dropdown menu. In the "Turn off monitor" dropdown menu, select "Never". Click the "Apply" button at the bottom of the window to confirm the changed settings. To close the window, click "OK". To finish the task, close the "Display Properties" window as well by clicking the "OK" button there as well.
